The Twelve LockGlyph Pack


This is a LockGlyph pack that consists of 12 main icons and 3 additional icons. It follows the theme of supernatural abilities such as teleportation, fire, light and such.

The 12 main symbols were used by the K-Pop group EXO for their debut concept.

Works With: LockGlyph, LockGlyph X
