About

Hi, I'm BBS. A computer science student who appreciates the endless customizations you can do to a jailbroken iPhone. I'm mainly interested in design, and that's why you find me mostly theming stuff.

Though I can't promise fulfillment, I'm open to receiving requests, especially LockGlyph ones. So, don't hesitate to contact me for requests!
